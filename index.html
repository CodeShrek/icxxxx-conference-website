About the Organizers & Partners

This inaugural edition marks a significant milestone in our journey toward fostering global academic collaboration and technological advancement.

Faculty of Technology

As the premier engineering and technology faculty at DU, we are dedicated to building the future through cutting-edge research, innovative pedagogy, and a forward-looking approach to computational sciences.

University of Delhi

Established in 1922, the University of Delhi is recognized globally for its academic excellence. It serves as the historic and intellectual foundation for this international gathering.

Springer Nature

We are proud to collaborate with Springer Nature. All accepted and presented papers for this first edition will be published in their prestigious proceedings, ensuring global reach and indexing.
